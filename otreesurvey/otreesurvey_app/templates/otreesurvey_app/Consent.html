Dear participant,

The purpose of this study is to investigate how people adapt their beliefs and behaviors about different issues. Your participation will involve an interview about meat-eating habits and motivativations with a Large Language Model (LLM, OpenAI GPT-4). The same LLM will try to summarize your habits and motivations. The study is conducted by researchers from the Complexity Science Hub. Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary and you may withdraw at any time.

The study will have two waves, one now (Dec. 9th) and one in a week (Dec. 16th). The first wave will take 30-35 minutes and you will receive 7 USD. The second wave will take 50-55 minutes and you will receive 15 USD. You might be invited for an additional third wave in January. You may refuse or redraw your consent at any time.

You are being asked to take part in this study because you are at least 18 years old. This study has been reviewed by the University of New Mexico ethics committee (UNM IRB 2501179238A001) and by the ethics committee of the Technical University of Vienna.

There are no foreseeable risks associated with this study, but some individuals may experience discomfort. You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. You may also ask us to remove your data from the study. Study participation is not expected to have any other impacts on your lifestyle. If you have any negative experiences during the study, please contact the study coordinators Mirta Galesic and Victor Møller Poulsen at collectivemindslab@gmail.com .

All metadata collected in this study that could identify you—such as your Prolific ID—will be kept strictly confidential and will only be disclosed with your explicit permission. This identifying information is stored separately from your survey responses and will not be included in any published or shared data.

When using LLM to summarize your responses, your data might be stored for up to 30 days on OpenAI servers for abuse monitoring. Your platform ID will only be used for compensation and will not be connected to your answers. Please do not include any personal, sensitive, or identifiable information in your written responses (e.g., names, addresses, or references to specific people or events that could reveal your identity).

Your fully anonymized data may be analyzed and shared in an Open Data Repository, accessible to other researchers for non-commercial, scientific use. This complies with EU data protection policies and supports transparency and reproducibility in academic research. Aggregated or anonymized findings may also be published or shared to support scientific conclusions.

If you complete the study but decide that you want your data removed, please contact the study coordinators Mirta Galesic and Victor Poulsen at collectivemindslab@gmail.com within 30 days after the survey completion.

I have read the information provided above and have had the opportunity to get answers to all relevant questions. By clicking “I consent” I indicate my voluntary agreement to participate in this research study.
